CoDEC
Collaborative Design for Environment & Cognition.

ISCO lab is an innovative and cutting-edge research hub dedicated to advancing the field of interior architecture through interdisciplinary collaboration, experimentation, and critical inquiry. Our lab brings together experts from diverse fields such as architecture, design, healthcare, engineering, and psychology to explore new approaches to designing, creating, and inhabiting interior spaces. At our lab, we are committed to pushing the boundaries of traditional interior architecture by integrating emerging technologies, sustainable practices, and user-centered design methodologies. Our research focuses on a wide range of topics, including adaptive reuse based on human perception and behavior. Through our research projects, publications, and collaborations with industry partners, we strive to contribute to the development of innovative and sustainable interior architecture solutions that enhance the human experience and respond to the ever-evolving needs of our society. Join us in exploring the possibilities of interior architecture and creating a more resilient and sustainable future for our built environment.
